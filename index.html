Back Pain Solutions in Faversham, Kent

Swale Back Pain Clinic treat a range of back related conditions and injuries. Our goal is to ease your pain and improve your quality of life.

Why Swale Back Clinic?

At Swale Back Pain Clinic our passion is relieving our clients back and neck pain. We've been helping people with back and neck pain for over twenty years along with joint pain, sports injuries and movement problems which often go hand in hand. The clinic is led by highly experienced Osteopath Lawrence Bergstrom.

We provide a full range of manual therapy treatment including Osteopathy, massage therapy, acupuncture and the unique IDD Spinal Therapy in addition to sports injury management, rehabilitation and bike fitting for cyclists in Faversham. Our commitment is to help patients avoid the need for pain medication and when it comes to back and neck pain, our goal is to help people avoid invasive treatments such as injections or surgery wherever possible.
